About Me

Research

Published researcher in astrophysics at UT Arlington's CDSLab. My work focuses on gamma-ray burst statistics, Bayesian inference, and correcting selection biases in observational astronomy datasets. Six peer-reviewed publications across A&A, ApJ, MNRAS, and Universe.

Data Science

Applying the same statistical rigour from research to real-world data problems — unsupervised ML, multi-source data pipelines, dimensionality reduction, and policy-oriented analytics. Currently building portfolio projects that span public health, urban safety, and geospatial analysis.

Urban Safety Atlas

Featured

County-level risk profiling across 3,100+ US counties. Merges four federal datasets (EPA, CDC PLACES, NHTSA FARS, Census SAIPE) into a composite safety index, then applies K-Means and HDBSCAN clustering to surface distinct safety tiers. Includes an interactive Streamlit choropleth dashboard with weight presets and county drill-down.
