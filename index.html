Urban Safety Atlas

Featured

County-level risk profiling across 3,100+ US counties. Merges four federal datasets (EPA, CDC PLACES, NHTSA FARS, Census SAIPE) into a composite safety index. Applies K-Means and HDBSCAN clustering to surface distinct safety tiers, with a Streamlit choropleth dashboard featuring weight presets and county drill-down.

Graph Neural Net: Neutrinoless Double Beta Decay

Research

Built a GNN classifier on 500K 3D point cloud files (100+ GB) to distinguish signal vs. background events in neutrinoless double beta decay searches, achieving 0.97 AUC on a balanced dataset. Engineered radius-based and k-nearest neighbor graph topologies using torch-cluster; trained across 6 GPUs with early stopping. Optimized classification threshold via Youden's J statistic.

PyTorch GNN torch-cluster Multi-GPU Point Clouds ROC / AUC

Bayesian Redshift Estimation for 1,366 GRBs

Research

Estimated unknown redshifts for 1,366 gamma-ray bursts using a multilevel hierarchical Bayesian model implemented in Fortran. Deployed MPI-based distributed parallelism on the TACC supercomputer to make computation tractable at scale. Published in ApJ; model provides a framework for population-level inference on incomplete GRB catalogs.

Bayesian Inference Fortran MPI TACC HPC Hierarchical Modeling
